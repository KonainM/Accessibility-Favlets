Installation Instructions

  • Chrome, Safari or newer versions of Firefox: Drag the Favlet link to your browser's bookmarks bar.
  • IE or older versions of Firefox: Right click or press the applications key on the Favlet link. Choose Add to Favorites, Add to Bookmarks or Bookmark this Link to add this Favlet to your Favorites or Bookmarks.
